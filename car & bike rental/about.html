Who We Are

Welcome to My Car & Bike Rentals – your trusted travel partner for hassle-free rides. We provide a wide range of well-maintained cars and bikes for daily, weekly, and long-term rentals. Whether you’re a tourist exploring new places, a business traveler in need of a reliable vehicle, or simply someone who wants a weekend getaway, we’ve got the perfect ride for you.

Our mission is to make travel affordable, flexible, and convenient. With doorstep pickup and drop, easy booking via WhatsApp, and 24/7 support, we ensure that your journey is smooth and memorable. Choose from a variety of options – from scooters for city rides to SUVs for family trips – and experience the freedom of traveling at your own pace.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to make travel simple, stress-free, and accessible to everyone. We aim to provide high-quality vehicles at affordable rates, ensuring that you enjoy every journey without worrying about transport.
